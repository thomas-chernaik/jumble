How to play

The aim of the game is to solve the jumble by rearranging the letters to form the correct word.

You will recieve 6 attempts to solve the jumble, and recieve feedback on each attempt.

A correct letter will be highlighted in light blue, a letter in the correct word but in the wrong position will be highlighted in yellow, and an incorrect letter will be highlighted in red.

Good luck!
